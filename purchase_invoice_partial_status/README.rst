This module extends the purchase order functionality in Odoo by introducing a new invoicing status: 'Partially Invoiced'. 

**Table of contents**

.. contents::
   :local:

Usage
=====


Once installed, this module automatically updates the invoice status of purchase orders based on their received and invoiced quantities.

- **Nothing to Bill** (`no`): No invoiceable lines exist.
- **Waiting Bills** (`to invoice`): There are products ready to be invoiced.
- **Fully Billed** (`invoiced`): The entire purchase order has been invoiced.
- **Partially Invoiced** (`partially_invoiced`): Some products have been invoiced, but not the entire purchase order.
